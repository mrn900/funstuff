Privacy Policy

Belvoir Build Group (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, partners, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for sourcing and construction services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information such as name, email, and phone number.
  • Product inquiries and messages sent through our website or WhatsApp.
  • Website usage data, including pages visited and interactions.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate product quotes and sourcing solutions.
  • To communicate important updates regarding your orders or inquiries.
  • To improve our website and services.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared with trusted partners only for the purpose of fulfilling your requests or improving service quality.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of our website may not function correctly.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us via WhatsApp or email at info@belvoirbuildgroup.co.zw.
